## Dialogs

Use <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.Msg> to show simple message boxes and <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.ErrorBox> to report exceptions to the user.

<xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.InputBox> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.OutputBox> (along with <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.OutputGridBox> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.OutputTreeBox>) provide quick prompts and result displays.

Derive your own dialogs from <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.OKCancelDialog> for consistent OK/Cancel behavior.

## Controls

<xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.FilteredTreeView`1> displays a list of <xref:NanoByte.Common.INamed>s in a tree with incremental search.

<xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.TaskControl>, <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.TaskProgressBar> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.TaskLabel> display the progress of <xref:NanoByte.Common.Tasks.ITask>s.

<xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.HintTextBox>, <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.UriTextBox> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.Controls.DropDownButton> provide specialized variants of standard WinForms controls.
