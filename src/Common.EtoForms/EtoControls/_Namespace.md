## Dialogs

Use <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.Msg> to show simple message boxes and <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.ErrorBox> to report exceptions to the user.

<xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.InputBox> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.OutputBox> (along with <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.OutputGridBox> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.OutputTreeBox>) provide quick prompts and result displays.

## Controls

<xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.FilteredTreeView`1> displays a list of <xref:NanoByte.Common.INamed>s in a tree with incremental search.

<xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.TaskControl>, <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.TaskProgressBar> and <xref:NanoByte.Common.EtoControls.TaskLabel> display the progress of <xref:NanoByte.Common.Tasks.ITask>s.
